Our story

Not all heroes wear capes. Some prefer aprons

With the pandemic putting many out of work and closing many of our favourite restaurants, we've discovered a network of home-cooks and independent vendors making ends meet selling their authentic food. We are now on a mission to help them share their talent with a wider audience whilst making a sustainable income online.
